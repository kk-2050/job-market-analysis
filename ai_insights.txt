# Job Market Analysis: 3 Key Insights

## 1. **Key Trend: Mid-Senior Level Dominance**
The job market is heavily concentrated at the Mid-Senior level, with **11,450 positions** (nearly 45% of all openings). This indicates strong demand for experienced professionals with 5-10+ years of background, suggesting the market values proven expertise and established track records.

## 2. **Salary Insight: Significant Jump at Leadership Levels**
There's a dramatic **salary acceleration in leadership roles**:
- Entry Level: ~$46K
- Mid-Senior Level: ~$101K
- Director+: ~$193K-$224K

This represents a **4-5x multiplier** from entry to executive level, showing that career progression directly correlates with substantial compensation increases.

## 3. **Recommendation for Job Seekers**
**Strategy depends on your stage:**
- **Entry-level candidates**: Accept that initial roles are plentiful but lower-paid (~$46K). Focus on skill development to reach Mid-Senior level quickly—that's where both job availability and salary jump significantly.
- **Mid-career professionals (5-10 years)**: You're in the "sweet spot" with abundant opportunities. Leverage your position to negotiate or transition into Director-track roles to access the 2x salary increase.
- **Career accelerators**: If advancing to leadership is your goal, note that Director positions are rarer (1,200 vs 11,450 at Mid-Senior), making them more competitive but substantially more rewarding.
